Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At SIP&GO!, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our drinking game application.

1. Information We Collect

SIP&GO! is designed to be privacy-first. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Data: We do not require accounts, names, emails, or personal information
  • No Game Data: Player names and game sessions are stored only locally on your device
  • Basic Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

2. How We Use Information

Any anonymous data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand which features are most popular

3. Data Storage

All game data (player names, current games) is stored locally on your device using browser storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be deleted by clearing your browser data

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking (PostHog)
  • Error Monitoring: Crash reporting to improve stability (Sentry)
  • Hosting: Content delivery and app hosting

These services have their own privacy policies and we ensure they meet our privacy standards.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser storage to:

  • Remember your language preference
  • Save your current game state
  • Store app preferences and settings

No tracking cookies or advertising cookies are used.

6. Children's Privacy

SIP&GO! is not intended for users under the legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 21 years old (or legal drinking age in your jurisdiction).

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we handle:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Minimal data collection practices
  • Regular security updates

8. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal personal data, you have the right to:

  • Use the app without providing personal information
  • Delete local data by clearing browser storage
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website.

Summary: SIP&GO! is designed to be privacy-first. We don't collect personal data, game data stays on your device, and we use minimal anonymous analytics to improve the app experience.
