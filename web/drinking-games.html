🍺 Classic Drinking Games

🏓Beer Pong

👥 4-8 Players ⏱️ 20-30 min 🎯 Skill

The ultimate party game combining skill, luck, and lots of fun. Teams take turns throwing ping pong balls into cups of beer.

How to Play:

  1. Set up 10 cups in triangle formation on each side
  2. Fill cups with beer (about 1/4 full)
  3. Teams alternate throwing balls into opponent's cups
  4. When a ball lands in a cup, the defending team drinks it
  5. First team to eliminate all opponent's cups wins

🔄Flip Cup

👥 6-12 Players ⏱️ 5-10 min ⚡ Fast-Paced

A fast-paced team relay game that gets everyone involved and creates amazing energy at any party.

How to Play:

  1. Divide into two equal teams
  2. Each player has a cup with beer
  3. First players drink and flip their cups
  4. Next player can't start until previous cup is flipped
  5. First team to flip all cups wins

🎲Never Have I Ever

👥 3-10 Players ⏱️ 15-30 min 💭 Social

Learn surprising things about your friends in this classic revelation game that's perfect for breaking the ice.

How to Play:

  1. Players sit in a circle with drinks
  2. Each player says "Never have I ever..." + something they haven't done
  3. Anyone who HAS done it takes a drink
  4. Continue around the circle
  5. Game ends when everyone's had enough fun!

🃏 Card-Based Games

👑Kings Cup

👥 4-8 Players ⏱️ 30-45 min 🃏 Cards

The king of drinking games! Each card has a rule, and the fun builds as the game progresses.

Basic Rules:

  1. Ace = Waterfall (everyone drinks)
  2. 2 = You (pick someone to drink)
  3. 3 = Me (you drink)
  4. 4 = Floor (last to touch floor drinks)
  5. King = Pour into center cup

🔢Ride the Bus

👥 4-10 Players ⏱️ 20-40 min 🎰 Chance

A game of luck and strategy where one unlucky player has to "ride the bus" at the end.

Game Phases:

  1. Red or Black? - Guess card color
  2. Higher or Lower? - Guess next card
  3. Between or Outside? - Guess if card falls between
  4. Suit? - Guess the suit
  5. Pyramid round with drinking rules
