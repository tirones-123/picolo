Responsible Drinking Guidelines

Responsible drinking means making informed choices about alcohol consumption while prioritizing your health, safety, and the safety of others. Here are essential guidelines to follow:

Playing SIP&GO! Safely

Our drinking game app is designed to be fun and social, but safety should always come first:

  • Modify Rules: Adjust penalty amounts to match your group's comfort level
  • Use Alternatives: Substitute alcoholic drinks with non-alcoholic options when needed
  • Take Breaks: Pause the game regularly to check in with everyone
  • Set Time Limits: Decide how long to play before starting
  • Respect Boundaries: No one should be pressured to drink more than they're comfortable with

Warning Signs to Watch For

Be aware of these signs that indicate someone has had too much to drink:

  • Confusion or disorientation
  • Vomiting or nausea
  • Slurred speech or difficulty speaking
  • Loss of coordination or balance
  • Difficulty staying awake or unconsciousness
  • Slow or irregular breathing
  • Cold, clammy skin or bluish skin color

Health Considerations

Before participating in drinking games, consider these health factors:

  • Medications: Many medications interact dangerously with alcohol
  • Medical Conditions: Certain health conditions make alcohol consumption risky
  • Pregnancy: Alcohol should be completely avoided during pregnancy
  • Age: The legal drinking age exists for important health and safety reasons
  • Mental Health: Alcohol can worsen depression, anxiety, and other mental health conditions

Alternatives for Non-Drinkers

SIP&GO! can be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of alcohol consumption:

  • Use mocktails or non-alcoholic beverages
  • Substitute drinking penalties with fun challenges
  • Focus on the social and entertainment aspects
  • Modify rules to include everyone comfortably
